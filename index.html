Who Am I?

I'm a crested gecko!

What is a crested gecko?

Crested Geckos are one of the largest gecko species, ranging from 8-10 inches, including 4-5 inches tail length. They are endemic to South Province, New Caledonia, but they are popular as pets, as they are small and easy to care for.
